About Henry

I am a passionate and experienced private tutor with over 6 years of teaching Mathematics, Science and English to students from JHS through University level. I hold a BSc in Mathematics from University of Ghana, Legon and have consistently helped students improve their grades by at least 2 letter grades within 3 months.

My approach is student-centered — I tailor every session to the individual learner's pace, learning style and goals. Whether you need exam preparation, remedial support or enrichment classes, I deliver results with patience and clarity.
