About TradeMate

Ghana's #1 Local
Services Platform

TradeMate is a digital marketplace that connects Ghanaians with trusted, verified local service professionals — from plumbers and electricians to tutors and photographers. We believe every person deserves access to reliable, quality help in their neighbourhood, without the frustration of referrals, no-shows, or overcharging.

Founded in Kasoa, we understand the unique challenges of the Ghanaian services market. Our platform was built ground-up to serve every region — from Accra to Tamale, Takoradi to Bolgatanga — making it easier than ever to discover, book, and pay for local expertise.

Meet the Founder

The vision behind
TradeMate

Effum Kofi Brebo is a Ghanaian entrepreneur and technology builder whose work sits at the intersection of community development and digital innovation. Raised in Kasoa, he grew up watching talented tradespeople — carpenters, electricians, plumbers — struggle to find consistent work despite their skill, while families spent hours chasing recommendations that often led nowhere.

"In Ghana, the talent has always been there. What was missing was the bridge — a trusted, efficient way for a skilled artisan in Tema to reach the family in East Legon who desperately needs their services."
— Effum Kofi Brebo, Founder & CEO

That observation became an obsession. Kofi spent years studying how digital platforms had transformed service economies in other parts of the world, then set out to build something designed specifically for Ghana — accounting for Mobile Money payments, regional languages, varying internet connectivity, and the deep importance of community trust.

Today, TradeMate serves 12,000+ verified providers across all 16 regions of Ghana, facilitating tens of thousands of bookings each month. Kofi continues to lead the company from Kasoa — the same community that first inspired him — with a team that shares his conviction that local work deserves a world-class platform.

Our Journey

From a local idea to a nationwide platform — key milestones.

💡
2021
The Idea Takes Root
Kofi identifies the trust gap between skilled tradespeople and households in Kasoa. Initial research begins — interviewing over 200 artisans and 150 families across Greater Accra.
🛠️
2022
TradeMate is Born
The first version of TradeMate launches in Kasoa and Accra Central with 47 verified providers across 8 categories. The MVP receives immediate traction from local homeowners.
📈
2023
National Expansion
TradeMate expands to all 16 regions of Ghana. Provider count crosses 5,000. Mobile Money integration launched, making bookings seamless for both customers and providers.
🌍
2024 – Now
12,000 Providers & Growing
Over 12,000 verified professionals. 45+ service categories. 4.9-star average rating. TradeMate becomes Ghana's most trusted local services platform — and the journey is only beginning.
